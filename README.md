HOW TO PLAY : 
1. Click paint button to produce paints in your bucket, and draw it to the map
2. use your middle mouse or the zoom control GUI to zoom in-out
3. press right click to move in any direction in the map display
4. to complete the domination; you need to place buildings units & annex bots until none of them were left behind; is suggested to place buildings & units to win.
5. upgrade your paint brush to win easily
6. have fun with loop gameplay
